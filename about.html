About the CDC Youth League

Building a brighter future for Liberia's youth through leadership, service, and unity under the banner of patriotism.

Who We Are

The CDC Youth League is the vibrant, youthful arm of the Congress for Democratic Change (CDC). We are dedicated to promoting the ideals of patriotism, integrity, and service among Liberiaâ€™s young population. Our mission is to inspire the youth to become future leaders who are deeply committed to the progress and prosperity of our nation.

What We Stand For

We stand for unity, social justice, and equal opportunities for every Liberian. Our initiatives are centered around empowering the youth to lead in their communities, contributing to national development, and championing the values that make Liberia strong.

Our Vision for the Future

Our vision is to nurture a generation of youth who are deeply connected to their roots, proud of their heritage, and committed to building a united and prosperous Liberia. We seek to inspire the youth to lead with integrity, courage, and a relentless pursuit of progress for all.

Empowering Liberia's Next Generation of Leaders

Through our training programs, community outreach, and political activism, the CDC Youth League is shaping the future of Liberia by cultivating youth leadership at all levels of society.
