CDC Youth League Retreat: National Youth Leadership Retreat

Thursday, October 29 – Sunday, November 3, 2024 | Buchanan City, Grand Bassa, Liberia

Introduction

The Congress for Democratic Change (CDC) has always stood at the forefront of social liberal democracy and grassroots mobilization, with a mission to advocate for equitable development, justice, and the empowerment of all Liberians. However, the 2023 electoral outcome has brought us to a pivotal moment, requiring a profound reassessment of our strategies, priorities, and internal cohesion. Our recent defeat was more than a loss; it was a signal for us to recalibrate our approach and heal the divisions that may have contributed to the outcome.

Now, as we prepare to forge a path back to power in 2029, the Revolutionary National Youth League (RNYL) is called upon to lead a process of renewal and reinvigoration. The RNYL represents the heartbeat of the CDC, embodying the energy, passion, and commitment needed to inspire a resurgence. This National Youth Leadership Retreat is not just another gathering; it is a call to action and a blueprint for transformation. From October 29 to November 3, 2024, youth leaders from across Liberia will gather in Buchanan City to initiate a process of reconciliation, strategic planning, and revitalization.

The retreat will commence with an opening ceremony attended by key dignitaries, invited national and international guests, and other stakeholders whose voices and experiences can inspire and align with our purpose. These guests will not only bring diverse perspectives but also lend their support to our renewed journey. Their presence is intended to invigorate our discussions, provide insights into overcoming similar challenges, and encourage us to set ambitious goals for the road ahead.

This retreat aims to accomplish more than just dialogue; it is about laying the foundation for a strong, united, and strategic movement that is equipped to reclaim national leadership in 2029. It will be a time to heal past divisions, reenergize our commitment to the CDC's core values, and collectively design a roadmap that will guide us back to power.

Purpose and Objectives

  1. Fostering Reconciliation - The primary goal is to cultivate a renewed sense of unity and solidarity within the National Youth League.
  2. Evaluating the 2023 Electoral Outcome - Understanding the factors that contributed to the electoral defeat is essential for charting a new course.
  3. Developing a Strategic Path Forward - With the 2029 elections in sight, the RNYL must define a clear and actionable roadmap.
  4. Recommitting to the CDC’s Vision and Values - The retreat will also serve as an opportunity to reaffirm our commitment to the CDC’s core values.
  5. Strengthening Organizational Structures - The RNYL must operate with optimal organizational capacity to effectively lead the party’s resurgence.

Thematic Scope

  1. Understanding the 2023 Electoral Loss and Extracting Lessons
  2. Reconciliation and Reinforcing Internal Unity
  3. Strategic Roadmap Development for the 2029 Elections
  4. Reaffirming the CDC’s Vision and Values

Expected Outcomes

  • Renewed Reconciliation and Unity
  • Strategic Agenda for 2029
  • Strengthened Leadership Commitment
  • Enhanced Organizational Capacity
  • Building Inclusive Political Alliances

The CDC National Youth League Retreat is not just a meeting—it is a pivotal turning point, an event that seeks to galvanize the movement toward a shared goal of resurgence. It will attract a host of national and international guests who can offer insights and inspire us to align our ambitions with practical strategies. Together, we will reignite the flame of progress, refine our strategies, and chart a course for reclaiming national leadership in 2029.
