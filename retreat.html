CDC Youth League Retreat: National Youth Leadership Retreat

Thursday, October 29 – Sunday, November 3, 2024 | Buchanan City, Grand Bassa, Liberia

About the National Youth League Retreat

The CDC National Youth League Retreat is not just a meeting—it is a pivotal turning point, an event that seeks to galvanize the movement toward a shared goal of resurgence. It will attract a host of national and international guests who can offer insights and inspire us to align our ambitions with practical strategies. Their perspectives will enrich the discussions, challenge us to think innovatively, and help crystallize our objectives. Together, we will reignite the flame of progress, refine our strategies, and chart a course for reclaiming national leadership in 2029. United in purpose and grounded in values, we will emerge from this retreat stronger, more resilient, and ready to shape Liberia’s future.

Purpose and Objectives

Our retreat is designed to unify and empower youth leaders for a brighter future. Here's what we aim to achieve:

  • Fostering Reconciliation

    The primary goal is to cultivate a renewed sense of unity and solidarity within the National Youth League.

  • Evaluating the 2023 Electoral Outcome

    Understanding the factors that contributed to the electoral defeat is essential for charting a new course.

  • Developing a Strategic Path Forward

    With the 2029 elections in sight, the RNYL must define a clear and actionable roadmap.

  • Recommitting to the CDC’s Vision and Values

    The retreat will also serve as an opportunity to reaffirm our commitment to the CDC’s core values.

  • Strengthening Organizational Structures

    The RNYL must operate with optimal organizational capacity to effectively lead the party’s resurgence.

Expected Outcomes

These outcomes reflect our goals for a stronger, united, and strategically driven organization.

Renewed Reconciliation and Unity

A stronger sense of solidarity and collaboration within the party.

Strategic Agenda for 2029

A clear and actionable roadmap guiding our preparations for the 2029 elections.

Strengthened Leadership Commitment

A renewed dedication among leaders to drive forward the CDC’s mission.

Enhanced Organizational Capacity

A more efficient, effective, and capable organizational structure.

Building Inclusive Political Alliances

Expanding partnerships for greater political inclusivity and support.
